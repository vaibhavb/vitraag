Deal Zero: DermaLens — where every advantage stacks

Secure vernacular derm-AI for tier-2 Maharashtra, built in the open

🩺 The concept

  • A patient in Nashik photographs and describes a skin condition — in Marathi.
  • DermaLens captures structured patient history so AI can triage severity and route to the right dermatologist.
  • Dermatology is the most AI-tractable specialty: visual, image-first, proven telederm models.
  • US teledermatology is the global expansion path.
  • Built and released open-source first — the residency's initial body of work, so the dermatology app has a foundation to develop on before any commercial layer is added.
  • The idea traces back to TimeLens, a personal side project — the "point your phone, see the layered history of a place" pattern is what pointed us toward "point your phone, see the layered history of a patient."
