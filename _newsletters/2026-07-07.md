The throughline this cycle is AI turning into an offensive and defensive tool at the same time — Cloudflare's Project Glasswing and Verizon's DBIR both point to adversaries using AI to find and exploit vulnerabilities faster, while Microsoft's RAMPART, OpenAI's Daybreak, and a wave of new agentic pentesting tools (AIRecon, Pentest Copilot, Visa's own vulnerability harness) are the defenders' answer. On the AI side, GEPA shows up twice as the optimization technique of the moment, agentic SDLC pieces from Optiver and a Kaggle whitepaper argue coding agents are reshaping how software actually gets built, and Ethan Mollick's 'Choosing to Stay Human' is worth sitting with as a counterweight.
